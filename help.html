Need Help?

1. Orders & Shipping

Q: How long does delivery take?

A: It usually takes 3–5 business days for delivery.

Q: Can I track my order?

A: Yes! We send a tracking link to your email after dispatch.

2. Returns & Refunds

Q: Can I return a product?

A: Yes, within 7 days if the item is unused and sealed.

Q: How will I get my refund?

A: Refunds are sent to your original payment method within 5–7 days.

3. Account & Login

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

A: Click on “Forgot Password” on the login page to reset it.

Q: How can I update my address?

A: Log in to your account → Go to Profile → Edit Address.
